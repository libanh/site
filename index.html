Research

My research interests lie in the area of machine learning and artificial intelligence, with a focus on developing algorithms and systems for large-scale data analysis and inference.

Some of my recent research projects include:

  • Developing scalable algorithms for Bayesian deep learning
  • Designing machine learning models for personalized medicine
  • Developing algorithms for fair and interpretable machine learning
