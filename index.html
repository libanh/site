Liban Hussein

Graduate Student in Electrical Engineering

About Me

I am a graduate student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Washington. My research interests include signal processing, machine learning, and computer vision. Before coming to UW, I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of California, Los Angeles.

Outside of research, I enjoy hiking and exploring the Pacific Northwest. I am also an avid reader of science fiction and fantasy.

Research

My current research focuses on developing deep learning algorithms for signal processing and computer vision applications. In particular, I am interested in using deep neural networks for image and audio classification, denoising, and enhancement.

As part of my research, I have also developed several open-source software packages for signal processing and machine learning, which are available on my GitHub page.

Publications
